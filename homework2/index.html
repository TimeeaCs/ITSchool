Physical Therapy Center

Taking good care of physical health is fundamental to our emotional health. In other words, our state of mind and thought processes are crucial to maintaining a healthy body and mind.
With its physical therapy and occupational therapy departments, the Treatment Center is fully equipped to provide patients of all ages with care delivered by our team of experts, who assess and treat all patients according to their individual goals and limitations in order to reduce illness and injury.
We rehabilitate both the body and mind to ensure relief for symptoms associated with injuries, ultimately aiming to restore patients to full health, as well as advising them on the most effective ways to reduce any potential injury recurrence.

Physical medicine and rehabilitation

Our diagnosis and individualized treatment procedures are carried out by our team of rehabilitative medicine specialists and other associated experts, meaning you can be confident in the seamless and continuous care delivered by this interdisciplinary team of professionals.

Pediatric physiotherapist can provide specialized physiotherapy treatment for children aged between 0 and 18 years that have difficulty with their physical development as a result of neurological and/or developmental problems. Neurological conditions can be as a result of problems with the brain, spinal cord or peripheral nerves. Babies and/or children with neurological conditions may have difficulties with their physical functions including mobility, muscle strength, range of movement and balance.

The Schroth Method is a nonsurgical option for scoliosis treatment. It uses exercises customized for each patient to return the curved spine to a more natural position. The goal of Schroth exercises is to de-rotate, elongate and stabilize the spine in a three-dimensional plane.
